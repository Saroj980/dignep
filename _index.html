Our Story

Born in Dhangadhi,
Built for Nepal

DigNep was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the digital divide in Nepal's western region and beyond. Based in Dhangadhi — the commercial heart of Sudurpaschim Province — we serve businesses, government bodies, and individuals who need reliable, professional IT services.

We believe that world-class technology should be accessible to every Nepali business, not just those in Kathmandu. Our team of certified engineers and developers works tirelessly to bring enterprise-grade solutions to organizations of all sizes across Nepal.

From setting up the first-ever enterprise Wi-Fi network in remote offices to deploying AI automation for growing businesses, DigNep has been at the forefront of Nepal's digital transformation journey.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you serve clients outside Dhangadhi and Sudurpaschim? +
Yes, absolutely. While we're headquartered in Dhangadhi, we serve clients across all of Nepal — from Kathmandu to Pokhara, and from Biratnagar to remote districts. We also provide remote support and consulting services internationally.
How quickly can you respond to a network emergency? +
For clients on our support plans, we guarantee response within 1 hour for critical issues, 4 hours for high-priority issues, and 24 hours for standard requests. Emergency on-site support in Dhangadhi is available within 2 hours.
What is the cost of a typical network setup? +
Network setup costs vary based on scope, number of devices, area coverage, and technology requirements. A typical small office network (10-20 users) starts from NPR 50,000. We always provide a free consultation and detailed quote before any commitment.
Do you offer annual maintenance contracts (AMC)? +
Yes, we offer comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for networks, servers, and IT infrastructure. AMC clients receive priority support, regular preventive maintenance visits, and discounted rates on equipment and services.
How long does web development typically take? +
A standard business website takes 2-4 weeks. E-commerce platforms typically require 4-8 weeks. Custom web applications can take 2-6 months depending on complexity. We provide realistic timelines upfront and keep you updated throughout development.
